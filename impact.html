Vice President of Marketing at IC Impact

Works as the Vice President of Marketing for IC Impact, a student run sustainability-based club at Ithaca College. As a club, we focus on bringing guest speakers to campus, and jumpstarting events and workshops that contribute to a more sustainable campus and community. IC Impact has started a chapter of Net Impact, a national organization that focuses on making a positive global impact, socially, environmentally etc. Marketing responsibilities include managing social media pages and marketing Sustainability Week, a week long series of sustainability workshops and events at Ithaca College. IC Impact plays a large role in organizing and marketing these events along with other key students, faculty members, and campus and community groups. In addition to marketing responsibilities, myself as well as other IC Impact members attended the Net Impact Conference in Phoenix Arizona in October of 2018. The conference included keynote presentations, panels, workshops, and other events with notable individuals, companies and organizations that are making a positive impact in the world. Myself and members had the opportunity to meet and network with other student leaders, as well as conference speakers.
